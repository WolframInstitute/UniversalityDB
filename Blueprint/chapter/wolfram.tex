\chapter{Connections to Wolfram Metamathematics}\label{ch:wolfram}

Wolfram~\cite{wolfram2022physicalization}: the ruliad as metamathematical space.
Our graph $\UG$ instantiates this for the domain of universality proofs:
vertices $\leftrightarrow$ objects, edges $\leftrightarrow$ proofs,
paths $\leftrightarrow$ composed proofs, graph metrics $\leftrightarrow$
metamathematical observables.

The Principle of Computational Equivalence predicts $\UG$ is highly connected.
