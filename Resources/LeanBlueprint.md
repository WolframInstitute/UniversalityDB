Reference example of a Lean Blueprint setup, from Terence Tao's PFR project.

Used as the template for this project's `Blueprint/` directory layout: `web.tex`, `print.tex`, `chapter/`, `preamble/` at the top level (no `src/` subfolder).

Upstream: https://github.com/pitmonticone/PFR (see `blueprint/` directory)
