# The Physicalization of Metamathematics and Its Implications for the Foundations of Mathematics

Stephen Wolfram, 2022.

Introduces the ruliad (the entangled limit of all possible computations), metamathematical space, and entailment cones. Proposes that mathematical theorems correspond to paths in this space, and that the structure of mathematics reflects the structure of the ruliad. The Universality Graph is a concrete instantiation: vertices are computational systems in the ruliad, edges are certified paths between them.
