# The Complexity of Small Universal Turing Machines: A Survey

Damien Woods and Turlough Neary, 2009. Theoretical Computer Science 410(4-5), 443-450.

Surveys the landscape of small universal Turing machines with detailed overhead analysis. Catalogs known UTMs by (states, symbols) pairs and the overhead required for each universality proof. Key reference for populating the Universality Graph with overhead bounds for small machine edges.

Download: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.tcs.2008.09.051
