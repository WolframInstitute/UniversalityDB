# Wolfram 2022 — Physicalization of Metamathematics

Stephen Wolfram. *The Physicalization of Metamathematics and Its Implications for the Foundations of Mathematics.* 2022.

## Summary

Introduces the ruliad — the entangled limit of all possible computations — and proposes a metamathematical space where theorems correspond to paths. The universality graph is a concrete instantiation of this idea: vertices are computational systems and edges are certified simulation paths through the space of computations.
